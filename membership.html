MENU

Mains

Chicken stroganoff with rice

Chicken stroganoff with rice is a delectable dish featuring tender chicken in a creamy sauce served over steamed rice. The flavors meld harmoniously, creating a comforting meal. Garnished with fresh parsley, it's a visual and culinary delight, offering both elegance and homely goodness on a single plate.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is an Italian classic that enchants with al dente pasta cradling a hearty meat sauce. Aromatic herbs and tomatoes infuse the sauce, resulting in a rich and satisfying dish. Timeless in its appeal, this comforting favorite delivers a symphony of flavors that soothe the soul and satisfy the senses.

Chicken, mash and broccoli

Chicken with mashed potatoes and broccoli is a well-rounded meal that brings together succulent chicken, velvety mashed potatoes, and vibrant broccoli. It offers comfort and nourishment in equal measure. This hearty combination is a feast for both the taste buds and the body, making it a satisfying choice for any meal.

Homemade Naan Pizza (Cheese)

Homemade Naan pizza is a delightful fusion of Indian and Italian cuisines. Crispy Naan bread serves as the canvas for a generous layer of melted cheese, creating a symphony of textures and flavors. It's a unique and mouthwatering take on traditional pizza, perfect for a quick, homemade indulgence that satisfies both the craving for comfort and culinary adventure.

Spaghetti ricotta, tomato and mozzarella pasta

Spinach and ricotta pasta is an elegant dish that combines vibrant spinach leaves with creamy ricotta cheese. Tossed with al dente pasta, it creates a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Grated Parmesan and cracked black pepper add gourmet touches, while a glass of white wine elevates the dining experience to a new level of sophistication.

Deserts

Fruit Parfaits

Delight in our Fruit Parfait, a harmonious blend of crunchy granola, creamy yogurt, and a medley of fresh, vibrant fruits like strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, and mango. Layered beautifully in a clear glass, it's a feast for the eyes and the palate. Ideal for a refreshing snack or a light dessert, our parfait is a celebration of natural flavors and textures.

No-Bake Cheesecake Jars

Savor the simplicity and elegance of our No-Bake Cheesecake Jars. Each jar layers luscious, creamy cheesecake atop a crumbly graham cracker base, crowned with a swirl of whipped cream and a fresh berry garnish. Perfect for any occasion, these jars offer a hassle-free yet decadent dessert experience, ready to delight with every spoonful.

Classic Tiramisu

Experience the timeless charm of our Classic Tiramisu. Layered with coffee-drenched ladyfingers and a velvety mascarpone cream, each bite offers a harmonious blend of bold and subtle flavors. Topped with a dusting of cocoa powder, this elegant dessert is a testament to Italian culinary art, perfect for indulging your sophisticated palate.
